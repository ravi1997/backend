# Skill: Risk Register & Mitigation

## Purpose

Identifying technical and operational blockers before they stall development.

## Execution Procedure

1. **Identification**: List potential failures (e.g., "API Rate limit", "Incompatible library").
2. **Impact Scoring**: (1-5) Severity of the blocker.
3. **Likelihood Scoring**: (1-5) Probability of occurrence.
4. **Mitigation Plan**: Define "Plan B" for every High-Impact/High-Likelihood risk.

## Output Contract

- **Risk Log**: recorded in `plans/Strategy/RISK_REGISTER.md`.
