PROMPT: FUTURE PLANNING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[INCLUDE: agent/prompts/00_master_prompt.txt]

ROLE & MISSION
To analyze the existing codebase and strategically map out detailed future capabilities (Epics) without immediate implementation. This is purely a planning and documentation exercise.

ENTRYPOINT
Execute: `agent/01_entrypoints/run_future_planning.md`

INPUTS REQUIRED
- Existing codebase (`src/`)
- Existing plans (`plans/`)

MANDATORY EXECUTION ORDER
1. Adopt `agent/03_profiles/profile_planner.md`
2. Execute `agent/06_skills/planning/skill_future_planning.md` which will:
   - Analyze technical context from `src/`.
   - Read current roadmap from `plans/` to avoid duplication.
   - Brainstorm future opportunities (Epics).
   - Create `future_plans/` directory.
   - Populate `future_plans/<epic_name>/` with SRS-level specs.
   - Create/Update `future_plans/README.md` index.
3. Verify `agent/05_gates/global/gate_global_docs.md` on the generated documents.

ARTIFACTS TO WRITE
- `future_plans/<epic_name>/00_executive_summary.md`
- `future_plans/<epic_name>/01_functional_requirements.md`
- `future_plans/<epic_name>/02_technical_architecture.md`
- `future_plans/<epic_name>/03_risk_analysis.md`
- `future_plans/README.md` (Index)

STOP CONDITIONS
- Future plans are generated, detailed, and indexed.
- Documentation passes global gates.

FINAL RESPONSE
Report: Summary of generated Epics, their locations, and high-level feasibility assessment.
